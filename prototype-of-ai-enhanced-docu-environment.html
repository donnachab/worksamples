DocBuilder: Concept Prototype

This proof-of-concept demonstrates a "Knowledge Graph" approach to Technical Documentation. It visualizes a workflow where documentation is treated as a database of semantic nodes rather than flat text files.

Schema-First Architecture

Every topic is wrapped in a governance schema defining its identity, owner, and graph edges. This ensures content is machine-readable and "AI-Ready" at the source.

Automated Governance

Simulates a "Docs-as-Code" pipeline where compliance and structural integrity are enforced before commitment, ensuring a "Single Source of Truth."

Architecture Note: This prototype uses vanilla JS/CSS to demonstrate the UI/UX concepts of a "Knowledge Writer's IDE" without backend dependencies.
